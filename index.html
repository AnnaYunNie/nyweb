My Life

A brief introduction about me

Yun Nie
English name: Anna

My parents gave me the name because they want me to overcome every difficulty as the vigorous grass "Yun". Since I came to the U.S, my friends like to call me, " Hey! Anna!"

I'm an international student from China. Due to my international background, I can easily make friends with those who come from other cultures and use different languages. I can speak Chinese, English, I also learn Japanese now.

I got my two Bachelor's Degree in Communication (2017) and Art (2013).

I got internship in China before graduation, working as a journalist and advertiser. To develop my technology skills, I designed several websites for individuals and non-profit groups, learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Photoshop, Lightroom, and other photo/video editing software. When designing this web, I made CSS grid as my basis for the layout, instead of traditional methods and Bootstrap. It is fun to learn more, explore awesome techniques, and try multiple ways to create.

After graduation, I began the dream to make my unique work marked as " Design-by-Yun".

I like to call myself a " knowledge hunter" because I often can't stop myself when I focus on something. My friends think I'm open-minded, outgoing, and never stop challenging myself.

Thanks for browsing! Enjoy my web!
