About Our Story

Our Group

We are SU students from the U.S, Japan, and China who aim at analyzing biased media portrayals of GLBTQ community, providing real information bout GLBTQ people, dealing with stereotypes, and creating a better life.

Our major is Communication Art. We collect useful information, launch academic research, and participate in activities related to GLBTQ community, media effect, and establishment of a better life with equality, harmony, and freedom. We hope you can enjoy this website and learn something that may help your relationship, work, and future life.
